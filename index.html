FireGuard

Revolutionizing approach to wildfire prediction and prevention with AI technologies
Recently, wildfires have been increasing in frequency and severity like never before, with the UN predicting the number of wildfires to increase by 50% by 2100. There is no doubt that with this staggering increase will come a pressing need to find alternative solutions to both prevent the start of wildfires and mitigate their detrimental effects, which is why our team seeks to find a solution to this using Artificial Intelligence.
